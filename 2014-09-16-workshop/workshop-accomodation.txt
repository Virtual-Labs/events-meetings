IIIT-Hyderabad has arranged for accommodation and meals of the participants of the Workshop Sept 16-20.
Accommodation is made at Bakul Boys Hostel and Parul Girls Hostel. The particiapnts can directly check-in at the respective hostels.
All 3 meals are arranged for the participants at NBH Mess. They just need to sign the register at the mess and have their meals. 
They need not pay for their meals.
